Soya bean oil

Soya bean oil is obtained from the soya plant and is the world’s most widely produced vegetable oil. The soya plant grows primarily in regions with a moderate climate and lots of water, such as in the USA, Brazil, Argentina and China.

Soya bean oil in its pure form is obtained by pressing the soya bean. The oil contains several times more saturated fat, linoleic acid and linolenic acids, but no cholesterol. This has a positive effect on human growth and also reduces the risk of heart attacks. The cholesterol level in the blood is also lowered by the lecithin and physterole contained in the soya beans.

Soya bean oil has a light yellow colour and a mild taste. Because it does not have a strong taste it is often used as a base for cooking oil mixtures. In addition to food products, soya bean oil is also used in bath oils and cosmetic products, such as in hair care products.
